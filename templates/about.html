Our Mission

Moodify was born from a simple idea: music is the language of emotions. Why should you have to search for songs when your face already tells the story?

The Technology

At its core, Moodify utilizes **Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs)**—a specialized type of Artificial Intelligence designed to process visual data. Our model has been trained on over **25,000 unique facial expressions** to achieve human-level accuracy in emotion detection.

"Music is the shorthand of emotion. We use technology to read that shorthand."

How it Works

Three simple steps to your perfect playlist.

1

Biometric Scan

Our system captures localized facial landmarks, focusing on the eyes, forehead, and mouth to map muscle contractions.

2

Emotional Mapping

AI classifies the input into seven primary emotions: Happy, Sad, Angry, Neutral, Surprise, Fear, or Disgust.

3

Vibe Generation

The detected mood is cross-referenced with musical tempo (BPM), key, and acousticness to deliver the ultimate song match.

The Psychology of Sound

Research shows that music affects the limbic system of the brain, the same area responsible for emotional processing. By matching the **rhythmic structure** of a song to your **current physiological state**, Moodify doesn't just play music—it creates an emotional resonance.
