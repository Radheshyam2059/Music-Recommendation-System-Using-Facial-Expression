Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. This includes:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password when you register.
  • Preferences: Your favorite artists and language settings.
  • Usage Data: Number of mood scans performed (total count only).

2. Biometric Data (Facial Expressions)

Our "Mood Detection" feature requires access to your camera. We do not store your images or videos on our servers. All facial analysis happens in real-time within your active session memory and is discarded immediately after the mood is detected. We only store the resulting "mood label" (e.g., "Happy") to provide recommendations.

3. Use of Cookies

We use cookies to keep you logged in and remember your theme preferences (Light/Dark mode). You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features of Moodify may not function correctly.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal information. Passwords are hashed using Bcrypt before being stored in our database.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@moodify.com or via our Contact Page.
